ABOUT ZYPHORIX

India's first digital Trading Card Game (TCG) platform — building a pro community where strategy, creativity, and competition collide!

Who We Are

Zyphorix is India’s premier digital TCG platform, founded by passionate gamers and developers. Our mission: to bring card gaming culture online, combine global eSports standards with Indian innovation, and power up a whole new generation of champions.

Our Vision

We believe card games should be fair, thrilling, and for everyone. Zyphorix is dedicated to fair play, competitive opportunities, and vibrant community spirit — where newcomers and pros can both shine.

Why Play Zyphorix?

  • Over 500+ unique, beautifully illustrated digital cards
  • Continous monthly tournaments, ranked ladders, and exclusive in-game rewards
  • Events, leaderboards, and feature-rich community tools
  • Full transparency: player data, security and anti-cheat first
  • Rising pro circuit: daily PvP, forums, and fan art contests

Meet the Team

We are a small but dedicated crew of developers, artists, and players — united by a love for TCGs. Our founders are card battlers, designers, and techies who wanted to create the experience they always dreamed of!
